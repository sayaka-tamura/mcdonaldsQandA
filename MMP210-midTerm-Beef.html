“Do you use ingredients which come from same country at all Macdonald around the world ?”
JiHye Y. from Toronto, ON

...See Answer

OUR ANSWER:

We can't speak for other countries, JiHye, but we source much of our food from right here at home! Over 80% of McDonald's Canada's annual expenditure is from suppliers in Canada. That includes 67 million pounds of 100% pure Canadian beef, 24 million litres of real milk and cream from Canadian dairy farmers, and 143 million pounds of potatoes harvested for our World Famous Fries.
